GENERAL VIEW:
Esc Quit
C   enter combat
M   move camera
V   View (inspect) tiles
I   Open inventory screen
S   Open stats screen
1/2 Set active slot
U   Use active

GENERAL VIEW, CONTEXT SENSITIVE:
T   Talk NPC (closest)
B   Barter NPC (closest)
O   Open container (on tile or closest)
E   Interact with thing (on tile/closest) (like buttons/consoles)

COMBAT:
C   exit combat, may not be possible
1/2 Set active slot
F   Fire weapon (active), enters targeting mode, show AP
U   Use active, AP
V   Inspect tiles/critters (no AP cost)
I   Open inventory (costs AP)
S   Open stats (no AP cost)
